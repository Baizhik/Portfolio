Hobbies

Hobbies are activities that individuals engage in for pleasure, relaxation, and personal fulfillment outside of their daily responsibilities. They allow people to explore new interests, learn skills, and express creativity.

Making sculptures

Sculpture making is an artistic process of shaping or combining materials to create three-dimensional forms. Sculptors work with materials like clay, stone, metal, wood, or more contemporary mediums such as recycled objects or digital techniques.

Painting

Painting is one of the most traditional and versatile forms of visual art. It involves applying pigments to surfaces such as canvas, wood, or paper, using tools like brushes, knives, or even hands. Artists can explore various styles and techniques, including realism, abstraction, impressionism, and expressionism.
